Italian Chicken Marinade

Description

Experience the vibrant flavors of Italy with our exquisite Italian Chicken Marinade. Succulent chicken, tenderized and infused with a harmonious blend of traditional Italian herbs and spices, awaits you. Our masterful combination of fragrant basil, robust oregano, and aromatic rosemary creates a symphony of flavors that dance on your palate.Prepared with the finest olive oil, our marinade not only enhances the natural juiciness of the chicken but also adds a touch of Mediterranean authenticity. The rich notes of garlic and tangy accents of lemon juice or vinegar perfectly balance the herbaceous profile, resulting in a tantalizing culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish; add chicken breasts and turn to coat. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess; discard remaining marinade.
  4. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 7 to 8 minutes on each side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
